Sevas at Sri Raghavendra Swamy Mutt

Sri Raghavendra Swamy Mutt offers various sevas and religious rituals every day. Devotees from across the country participate in these sacred services to seek the blessings of Sri Raghavendra Swamy. Every seva is performed with devotion according to the traditions of the Dvaita philosophy and Vedic scriptures.

🪔 Archana Seva

A special prayer performed by chanting the divine names of Sri Raghavendra Swamy for the well-being, prosperity, health, and happiness of devotees.

🌸 Tulasi Archana

Tulasi leaves are offered with devotion during worship, symbolizing purity, surrender, and devotion to Lord Hari.

🔥 Homa & Special Poojas

Sacred fire rituals and special poojas are conducted on auspicious days and festivals to invoke divine blessings and spiritual prosperity.

🍛 Annadanam

Free meals are served to thousands of devotees every day. Annadanam is considered one of the highest forms of charity and service in Hindu tradition.

🙏 Paduka Seva

Devotees offer prayers before the sacred Padukas of Sri Raghavendra Swamy, seeking his blessings for peace, success, and spiritual growth.

🎵 Bhajans & Vedic Chanting

Daily devotional songs and Vedic chanting create a peaceful spiritual atmosphere and inspire devotion among pilgrims visiting the Mutt.
